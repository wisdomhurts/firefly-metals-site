About FireFly Metals

An emerging copper-gold company focused on rapidly growing the resource base across three world-class projects.

Company Overview

FireFly Metals Ltd (ASX: FFM, TSX: FFM) is an emerging copper-gold exploration and development company with a portfolio of world-class assets. The Company's flagship Green Bay Copper-Gold Project in Newfoundland, Canada, hosts a significant and rapidly growing mineral resource, with ongoing drilling programs continuing to deliver exceptional results. FireFly is committed to responsible exploration, sustainable development, and creating long-term value for shareholders.

Our Strategy

FireFly Metals is focused on resource growth through aggressive and systematic drilling campaigns, conversion of inferred resources to measured and indicated categories, and establishing a near-term development pathway. The Company's strategy leverages its position in tier-one mining jurisdictions with established infrastructure and strong government support for responsible resource development.
